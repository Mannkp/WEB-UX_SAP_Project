Introducing HyperText Markup Language (HTML)

The following is a set of some of the most prominent features introduced in HTML5:

  1. New Semantic Elements: These are like <header>, <footer>, and <section>.
  2. Forms 2.0: These are improvements to HTML web forms, where new attributes have been introduced for the <input> tag.
  3. Persistent Local Storage: To achieve without resorting to third-party plugins.
  4. WebSocket: A next-generation bidirectional communication technology for web applications.
  5. Audio and Video: You can embed audio or video on your webpages without resorting to third-party plugins.
  6. Geolocation: Now visitors can choose to share their physical location with your web application.
  7. Drag and drop: Drag and drop the items from one location to another location on the same webpage.
